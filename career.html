Career Choices

Information Technology

Computer science is very important to the success of our students in a digital, inter-connected world. Computer science is a discrete academic discipline but is also embedded in virtually every area of academic study, since the use of computer technologies, data and digital storytelling have become essential skills in most disciplines

Sports

Sports have been viewed as a way to stay healthy and fit. But sports importance goes much further. Sports help in the overall development of students. Playing sports teaches lessons of life, such as teamwork, accountability, self-confidence, responsibility, and self-discipline.
